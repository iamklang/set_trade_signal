Bottom Line

All 8 ROTATE exits in Q3 had a hold time of 0-2 bars — every single one would be blocked by the min-hold guard alone. 3 of the 8 were also winners at exit, so the winner guard provides a second layer.

Of the 8 blocked trades: 6 went on to win (counterfactual P/L > 0 at latest), 1 still lost (ADVANC, but the loss shrank from -1.83% to -0.79%), and 1 stayed flat (BANPU).

The standout case is BCP.BK: rotated out at entry price on the same day, then rallied +13.33% over the next 4 bars. This single trade accounts for more than half of the total counterfactual gain.

Net verdict: guards are strongly positive (+3.07% avg counterfactual P/L across all 8 trades)
